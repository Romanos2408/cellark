# Cellar·K — Council Audit (2026-05-30)

9-dimension multi-agent audit (bugs, responsive, a11y, SEO, legal/cookies, i18n, links, perf,
missing). 52 findings confirmed after adversarial verification.

**Verdict:** Well-built and visually strong, but not launch-ready as a real commercial wine shop.
Genuine blockers cluster in two areas: (1) a CDN single-point-of-failure + a keyboard-inoperable
category filter; (2) missing Greek e-shop legal pages + business identification. Most other items
are pre-launch polish, since no transaction can occur until Shopify is connected.

## 🔴 Must-fix before launch

### A. Technical (Claude can fix now)
1. **CDN single point of failure** — GSAP/Lenis load from esm.sh at runtime, and the same module
   injects the wine grid + reveals hero copy. If esm.sh is slow/down, both pages render near-blank.
   → Vendor the libs locally into `assets/vendor/`, repoint the importmap, add `modulepreload`.
2. **Category filter tabs keyboard/screen-reader inoperable** — tabs are `<a>` with no href/role;
   keyboard users are stuck on "Whites" (~10/13 wines unreachable). WCAG 2.1.1/4.1.2.
   → Render as `<button role="tab">` in a `role="tablist"`, roving tabindex, arrow-key nav.
3. **Cart drawer has no focus trap / focus return** — opens on every add; keyboard users tab behind
   it. (age-gate.js already does this correctly — just mirror it.) WCAG 2.4.3.
4. **Partial Shopify variant map silently drops bottles** — if one variant ID is blank at launch,
   that wine vanishes from checkout with no warning. → guard in `shopifyCheckoutURL()`.
5. **`index.html#story` deep link lands at wrong scroll position** (Lenis desync w/ pinned section).

### B. Legal / business (needs your input — values + policy text)
6. **No Privacy Policy, Terms of Sale, or Returns/Withdrawal pages** — required for a Greek consumer
   e-shop before checkout goes live (GDPR; Law 2251/1994; CRD 14-day withdrawal). Hard blocker the
   moment Shopify connects. → Claude can scaffold bilingual pages; **you/accountant/lawyer supply the
   actual entity details + policy specifics** (or use Shopify's built-in policy generators).
7. **Footer lacks legal links + mandatory business identification** — Greek law (PD 131/2003, ΓΕΜΗ
   Law 4919/2022) requires legal trading name + ΑΦΜ + ΓΕΜΗ + ΔΟΥ shown. → add legal-links row +
   imprint line (**you supply the numbers**).
8. **Consent banner must ship BEFORE any analytics** — none today (so compliant now), but the instant
   GA4/pixels are added without a prior-opt-in gate it violates Law 3471/2006. Build the gate first.

- Smaller bottle thumbnails; lazy-load hidden story bottles.

- Global **:focus-visible** ring (only `.btn` has one today).

- **Keep the Catalogue link reachable on mobile** (currently hidden <680px with no hamburger).
- Harden cart drawer for short/landscape phones (100dvh, min-height:0, scrollable foot).
- Cross-tab cart badge sync; add-to-basket label language fix; reduced-motion guard on tab fade.
- Add "Απολαμβάνετε υπεύθυνα · 18+" at the checkout moment; add img width/height to stop reflow.

## 💡 Missing / strategic
- **Capture demand during "shop opening soon"** — let the cart's soon-state open a pre-filled enquiry
  email with the basket, instead of a dead end (turns pre-launch traffic into leads).
- **Shipping & delivery info** (areas, courier, cost, timeframe, breakage policy) — none exists.
- **Analytics** wired behind the consent gate (+ add_to_cart event) so the funnel is measured from day one.
- **Richer Story / Trade content** — biggest credibility gap for a premium importer courting restaurants.
- Bilingual OG image alt (polish). 404 absolute URLs to update at the cellark.gr cutover (tracked).
