# Cellar·K — CEO setup checklist

The website is built. These are the few things only the **business owner** can do
(account, payments, domain, legal/business details). Romanos handles the website,
product import, domain DNS, and wiring it all together.

> 💡 Tip: after step 1, **invite Romanos as a staff member** (Settings → Users → Add)
> so he can configure the store for you — you don't have to do the technical parts.

---

## 1. Create the Shopify account
- Go to **shopify.com → Start free trial** (no payment needed to start).
- Choose the **Basic** plan when asked (don't pay yet — the trial lets us build first).
- Set the store to **Greece / EUR / Greek language**.
- **Invite Romanos** as staff (Settings → Users and permissions → Add user).

## 2. Activate payments (owner-only — needs business + bank + ID)
- Settings → Payments → activate **Shopify Payments**.
- Have ready: **business legal name, ΑΦΜ, business bank account (IBAN), ID/φωτό ταυτότητας**.
- Add **Αντικαταβολή (cash on delivery)** as a manual payment method.

## 3. Buy the domain (recommended before public launch)
- Buy **cellark.gr** (~€10–20/year) — easiest **through Shopify**, or a Greek registrar (papaki.gr).
- That's it — **Romanos configures the rest** (pointing it to the site + the shop).

## 4. Hand these details to Romanos (for the legal pages + site)
- **Legal trading name**, **ΑΦΜ**, **ΓΕΜΗ number**, **ΔΟΥ**, registered address.
- Any **alcohol-trade / import licence** info, if applicable.
- The **Shopify store address** once created (e.g. `cellark.myshopify.com`).

## 5. Business decisions to provide
- **Retail prices** for the 13 wines (with ΦΠΑ).
- **Wholesale prices** (net, by the case) + any minimum order.
- **Shipping**: courier, delivery cost, free-shipping threshold, COD fee.

## 6. Ask the accountant
- Which **myDATA πάροχος** (e-invoicing provider) you use, so invoices are issued legally.
